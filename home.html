The Wexford Funfair in Courtown, Ireland, is an annual event offering thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, and a vibrant carnival atmosphere. Located by the sea, the funfair offers classic carnival games, delicious treats, and live entertainment. The event transforms into a wonderland of lights and colors as the sun sets, fostering community spirit and camaraderie.
